Pizza

Simple pizza

Ingredients

  1. Water, 1 Cup
  2. Sugar, 1 Tablespoon
  3. Yeast, 2.5 Teaspoons
  4. Salt, 1 Teaspoon
  5. Vegetable Oil, 1 Tablespoon
  6. Flour, ~2.5 Cups
  7. Toppings, Cheese, Sause, Garlic, Meat, etc.

Instructions

  1. Get water warm and mix it with yeast and sugar
  2. Wait a few minutes for yeast to activate
  3. Mix salt, then oil, then lastly flour
  4. You may need to put the dough on the counter to mix with your hands
  5. Dough should form into a consistant ball
  6. Leave for at least 30 minutes to rise
  7. Prehead oven to ~200 degrees
  8. On your pizza pan spread out the dough
  9. Leave pizza in oven until it's just hard enough to put toppings on
  10. Set oven to 425 degrees
  11. Cook for about 20 minutes
