Pizza

Simple pizza, ~$1.67


Ingredients

    Base, $0.37

  1. Water, 1 Cup
  2. Sugar, 1 Tablespoon, $0.01
  3. Yeast, 2.5 Teaspoons, $0.16
  4. Salt, 1 Teaspoon, $0.01
  5. Vegetable Oil, 1 Tablespoon, $0.04
  6. Flour, ~2.5 Cups, $0.15

    7. Toppings, $1.30

  7. Cheese, $0.43
  8. Sause, $0.25
  9. Pepperoni, $0.62

Instructions

  1. Get water warm and mix it with yeast and sugar
  2. Wait a few minutes for yeast to activate
  3. Mix salt, then oil, then lastly flour
  4. You may need to put the dough on the counter to mix with your hands
  5. Dough should form into a consistant ball
  6. Leave for at least 30 minutes to rise
  7. Prehead oven to 220 degrees
  8. On your pizza pan spread out the dough
  9. Leave pizza in oven for about 50 minutes
  10. Put toppings on pizza
  11. Set oven to 425 degrees
  12. Cook for about 18 minutes
