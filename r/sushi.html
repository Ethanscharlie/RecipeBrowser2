Sushi

Sushi rolls, ~$1.38


Ingredients

    Base Ingredients, $1.17

  1. Sushi Rice, 1 Cup, $0.99
  2. Water, 1 cup
  3. Nori (Seaweed Sheet), 1/2 sheet, $0.13
  4. Vinegar, 1/8 cup, $0.03
  5. Salt, A pitch, $0.01
  6. Suger, A pintch, $0.01

    7. Toppings (Optional)

  7. Crab Stick, 1 Stick, $0.21

Instructions

    Rice

  1. Wash rice 3 times
  2. Put rice in rice cooker with 1 cup of water for 2 cycles
  3. Mix Vinegar, salt, and sugar in bowl

    4. Rolling

  4. Take half of Seaweed sheet, and place rice mostly on the top section
  5. Put crab on top section
  6. Roll Sushi tightly
